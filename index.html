French Onion Soup

32 min   2 people     23 calories
Ingredients
• 1/2 cup unsalted butter • 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 4 cups sliced onions • 4(10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
• 2 tablespoons dry sherry • Salt and pepper to taste
• 4 slices French bread • 4 slices provolone cheese
• 2 slices Swiss cheese diced • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Instructions
• Melt butter with olive oul in an 8 quart stock pot on medium heat/ Add onions and hve the SAKI automatic Pot stirrer contintually stir until tender and translucent
